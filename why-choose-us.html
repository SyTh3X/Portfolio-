Why Winslow & Rye

Six reasons people order their second sofa from us too

Premium Quality

Kiln-dried timber frames, corner-blocked and doubled-jointed rather than stapled, with high-resilience foam and fibre-wrapped cushions that hold their shape for years rather than months. We'd rather a sofa cost slightly more and need replacing decades later than the other way round.

UK-Wide Delivery

A two-person team delivers to the room of your choice across the UK mainland, unpacks on site, and takes every scrap of packaging away with them. No wrestling a sofa up a staircase alone.

Handmade Craftsmanship

Every frame is corner-blocked, glued and screwed by hand, and every cover is cut and stitched by an upholsterer rather than a machine line. It's slower, and it's why a hand-tied spring set still feels right a decade in.

Personal WhatsApp Service

There's deliberately no checkout on this site. Every enquiry opens a real conversation with someone who can answer a question about fabric weight or lead times without putting you on hold.

Excellent Customer Service

The person who answers your first message is usually the same person who follows up about delivery day — no re-explaining your order to a new agent each time.

Custom Orders Available

A mirrored chaise, a different fabric to the one shown, a slightly longer run — most of our range can flex. Ask when you message us and we'll tell you honestly whether it's possible.
