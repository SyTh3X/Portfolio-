Privacy Policy

Template last updated: 16 July 2026

1. Who we are

This website is operated by Winslow & Rye ("we", "us", "our"). We are the data controller for the personal data described below. You can reach us using the details on our Contact page.

2. This website does not have a checkout or account system

Winslow & Rye works as a product catalogue. There is no shopping cart, no account creation, and no payment taken on this website. Because of this, the site itself does not operate a customer database — browsing the catalogue does not, by itself, send any personal data to us.

3. What happens when you press "Contact on WhatsApp"

Every product page includes a button that opens WhatsApp with a message already containing details of the sofa you were viewing (name, price, your selected colour and a link back to that page). Pressing the button does not send anything automatically — WhatsApp opens with the message pre-filled, and it is only sent once you press Send inside WhatsApp.

Once sent, that message is transmitted through WhatsApp, a service operated by WhatsApp Ireland Limited (part of Meta). WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs how the message is transmitted and secured in transit. We then receive it as the intended recipient, in the same way as any other WhatsApp conversation.

4. What personal data we collect, and why

When you message us — by WhatsApp, the contact form (which also opens WhatsApp), phone or email — we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number (visible to us via WhatsApp)
  • Any details you choose to share, such as your address, email, or preferences about a sofa
  • The content of your messages to us, so we can respond and keep a record of your order

We use this information to respond to enquiries, agree and process orders, arrange delivery, and meet our legal obligations (for example, keeping records for accounting purposes).

5. Our legal basis for processing

We rely on contract (to take and fulfil your order), legitimate interests (to respond to enquiries and improve our service), and legal obligation (for example, accounting and tax records) as our bases for processing personal data.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry and order information for as long as is reasonably necessary to fulfil your order, deal with any after-sales issue, and meet our legal and accounting obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

7. Who we share data with

We may share necessary details (such as your name, address and phone number) with our delivery partners, so your sofa can be delivered, and with payment processors where relevant to your order. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

8. Cookies and analytics

This template does not include any analytics or advertising cookies by default. If you later add tools such as Google Analytics, update this section to describe what is collected and provide a cookie consent banner where required under UK PECR rules.

9. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been mishandled.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it was last revised.

11. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to or via WhatsApp using the button on this page.
